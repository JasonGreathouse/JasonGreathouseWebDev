Chelsea FC Championships

Chelsea Football Club have won Premiere League title 5 times: 2004, 2005, 2009, 2014, and 2016.
Chelsea have also won the most prestigious club competition, The Champions League, twice: 2012, and 2021.

Didier Drogba

Didier Drogba

Chelsea Icon Didier Drogba was among the top goal scorers in ChelseaFC history with 164 goals in 381 appearances.
Didier Drogba was a critical goal scorer in 4 of Chelsea's premiere league title seasons, 1 Champions League, and was the winner of 2 Golden Boot Awards.

Stamford Bridge

More Info

The home stadium for Chelsea FC is Stamford Bridge, located in West London.
Currently, the stadium seats 40,431 people and cost nearly 1.6 billion pounds.
