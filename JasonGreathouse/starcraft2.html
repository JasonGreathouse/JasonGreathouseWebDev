StarCraft 2

Released July 2010

Real-Time Strategy Game by Blizzard Entertainment

StarCraft 2 is a real-time strategy game that involves resouce management, base building, and army control to battle opponents.
The game provides three races or factions to chose from. The original game delves into the Terran's lore, while the expansions cover the other two races. Heart of the Swarm, the first expansion, dives into the lore of the zerg race and its characters. Legacy of the Void, the final expansion, provides lore on the Protoss race.


Starcraft Factions

Terran
Zerg
Protoss

The Terran race, led by Jim Raynor, brings the firepower! Heavy artillery delivers devastating blows, while soldiers and medics push the frontline. This faction is well-rounded and excels at controlling the battlefield

The Zerg, commanded by Sarah Kerrigan, leverage speed and overwhelming numbers! They quickly swarm opponents, striking from multiple angles at once. True to their name, The Swarm, this faction is devastating when left unchecked

The Protoss carry an unwavering pride, led by Artanis and armed with advanced technology! Anywhere can become a battlefield thanks to their teleportation tactics, and any fight is winnable with the right army composition. This faction easily dismantles enemies with starships and high-tech weaponry.
